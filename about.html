About FORAY Protocol

Privacy-preserving blockchain audit infrastructure for enterprise transactions

What is FORAY?

FORAY Protocol creates tamper-evident audit trails for enterprise transactions while keeping sensitive business data private. The name derives from "4A" - the four fundamental components that every transaction decomposes into:

  • Arrangements - Contracts, terms, and counterparty agreements
  • Accruals - Formulas applied against asset flows (interest, depreciation, allocations)
  • Anticipations - Expected future cash flows and deliverables
  • Actions - Actual asset transfers and settlements

By structuring transactions this way, FORAY enables cryptographic verification without exposing proprietary business logic or competitive intelligence.

Kaspathon 2026

FORAY Protocol is being developed as a submission for Kaspathon 2026 in the Real-Time Data Track. The hackathon showcases innovative uses of Kaspa blockchain technology.

Kaspa's unique blockDAG architecture provides sub-second finality and extremely low transaction costs - ideal for enterprise audit anchoring where thousands of transactions may need to be recorded daily.

Target Use Cases

Defense Contractors

DCAA compliance, cost accounting, labor hour tracking, and contract management with cryptographic proof of integrity.

Financial Services

SOX compliance, Basel III requirements, derivative valuations, and complex multi-party transactions.

Manufacturing & Supply Chain

Provenance tracking, work order management, inventory movements, and supplier payments.

Energy Sector

Power purchase agreements, renewable energy credits, meter readings, and settlement calculations.
