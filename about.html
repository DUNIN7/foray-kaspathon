What is FORAY?

FORAY Protocol is a privacy-preserving blockchain audit infrastructure designed to create immutable transaction records for enterprise systems. The name is derived from "4A" ‚Äî the four components that form the core of every transaction: Arrangements, Accruals, Anticipations, and Actions.

FORAY solves a fundamental problem in enterprise auditing: how to prove that financial transactions occurred correctly without exposing sensitive business data. By separating cryptographic proofs (anchored on-chain) from business data (stored off-chain), FORAY enables blockchain's immutability benefits while preserving competitive secrets.
