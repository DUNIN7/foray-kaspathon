FORAY Protocol: Structured Truth for Enterprise Transactions

Enterprises struggle to prove that complex financial transactions occurred correctly, with full auditability. FORAY solves this by decomposing any transaction into four verifiable components - Arrangements, Accruals, Anticipations, and Actions - creating a tamper-evident audit trail anchored to the Kaspa blockchain.

THE PROBLEM: AUDIT TRAILS ARE FRAGMENTED

Disconnected Records

Contracts live in one system, calculations in spreadsheets, payments in another - auditors must reconstruct the full picture manually.

THE FORAY SOLUTION: 4-COMPONENT STRUCTURE

Every transaction decomposes into four linked components that create a structured chain from contract to settlement.

1. ARRANGEMENTS

Contractual Setup

  • Parties involved and jurisdictions
  • Terms and conditions
  • Effective dates
  • Legal documentation

2. ACCRUALS

Computational Truth

  • Formula calculations (hashed)
  • Interest, depreciation, allocations
  • Accounting entries (debit/credit)
  • Fiscal period tracking

3. ANTICIPATIONS

Forecasted Obligations

  • Expected payment amounts
  • Probability factors
  • Due dates and schedules
  • Assumptions documented

4. ACTIONS

Settlement Events

  • Actual payment execution
  • Allocations with tracking
  • Variance analysis (expected vs actual)
  • Settlement confirmation
