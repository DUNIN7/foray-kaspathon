FORAY Protocol creates tamper-evident blockchain audit trails for enterprise transactions without exposing sensitive business data. Powered by Kaspa's sub-second block times.

The Problem

Enterprise Audit Trails Are Broken

Companies spend billions annually on audits, struggling to prove that complex financial transactions occurred correctly.

Fragmented Records

Contracts in one system, calculations in spreadsheets, payments in another. Auditors must reconstruct the full picture manually.

Delayed Detection

Problems are discovered months or years later during periodic audits. By then, evidence may be lost or corrupted.

Privacy vs. Transparency

Full transparency exposes competitive secrets. Full privacy prevents verification. Finding the right balance has been difficult.

Mutable Records

Traditional systems allow backdating and alteration. Even "audit logs" can be modified by database administrators.

The Solution

The 4A Model + Attestations

FORAY decomposes business transactions into four auditable components, with optional third-party attestations for enhanced trust.

Arrangements

Contractual foundations defining parties, terms, and obligations. The "what we agreed to."

Accruals

Formulas applied against asset flows. The "how amounts are computed."

Anticipations

Expected future cash flows and deliverables. The "what we expect to happen."

Actions

Actual asset transfers and settlements. The "what actually moved."

+ Attestations Optional Extension

Third-party verifications from laboratories, auditors, certification bodies, or regulators. Creates trust chains for supply chain provenance, product authentication, and regulatory compliance.
